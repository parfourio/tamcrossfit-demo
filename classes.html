CrossFit Classes

Coach-led group training. Every level, every day. Come as you are - leave stronger than you arrived.

The Structure

HOW A CLASS WORKS

Every TCF class runs 60 minutes and follows the same tested structure. Whether it's your first day or your thousandth, you'll always know what to expect - and always be pushed beyond what you thought you could do.

01
Step 01
Warm-Up
Dynamic movement prep, mobility work, and nervous system activation. Your coach walks you through everything - we protect your body first.
⏱ 10 minutes
02
Step 02
Skill & Strength
Focused technique work or a strength piece. Olympic lifting, gymnastics, barbell cycling - our certified coaches ensure perfect form before you load weight.
⏱ 15-20 minutes
03
Step 03
The WOD
The Workout of the Day. Timed, scored, and endlessly scalable. Your coach modifies every movement so you're always working at the right intensity for your level.
⏱ 10-20 minutes
04
Step 04
Cool-Down
Structured recovery - stretching, mobility, and breathing. We score the WOD, talk through technique, and celebrate what you just accomplished together.
⏱ 10 minutes

The Science

10 FITNESS DOMAINS

CrossFit's programming is built around developing competency across ten recognized physical skills. This isn't random - it's the most comprehensive approach to general physical preparedness ever developed.

At TCF, our programming intentionally cycles through all ten domains so you never have a glaring weakness. The result is fitness that carries over into real life, sport, and whatever challenge comes next.

"Our goal is well-rounded, functional fitness. Not just looking strong - being strong, mobile, and ready for anything."

Inside Every Class

WHAT YOU'LL EXPERIENCE

🎓
Expert Coaching
Every class is led by a certified CrossFit coach who knows your name, your goals, and your limitations - and is watching your form on every rep.
🔄
Infinite Scalability
Every single movement and workout is scaled to you. Your version of the WOD is as legitimate as anyone else's. There's no "modified" - just your level.
📊
Trackable Progress
WOD scores, PRs, and benchmark tests let you see actual improvement over time. The numbers don't lie - and watching them climb is addictive.
🤝
Community Accountability
Your class holds you accountable. People notice when you miss. When you show up, they cheer. That's not pressure - that's the secret ingredient.
🔥
Constantly Varied
No two days are exactly the same. You'll never plateau because your body never fully adapts. Every day is a new challenge, a new skill, a new win.

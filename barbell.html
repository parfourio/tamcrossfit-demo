San Rafael
Barbell

San Rafael's premier strength and weightlifting gym. No-nonsense. Well-equipped. Capped memberships so you always have room to lift.

24/7 Access Included with Every Membership
What It Is

SKIP THE CROWDS.
KEEP THE IRON.

San Rafael Barbell is a clean, well-equipped strength training space with capped memberships - so you always have room to lift. Every membership includes 24/7 door code access. Train on your time.

Located next door to Tamalpais CrossFit, this is for the serious strength athlete who follows their own programming, wants competition-grade equipment, and doesn't need someone telling them what to do every rep.

"If classes aren't your thing but you still want serious equipment and a respectful community - welcome home."
Who It's For

The Independent Lifter

San Rafael Barbell is for athletes who follow their own strength or weightlifting program and need a serious facility to execute it. You don't need coached WODs - you need rack space, good bars, and a space where people respect the iron as much as you do.

Next Door To Classes

Tamalpais CrossFit is right beside us. San Rafael Barbell members can add coached CrossFit classes anytime. One building, two ways to train.

Why Lifters Love It

BUILT FOR PEOPLE WHO LIFT.

24/7 Access Included - Personal Door Code
Every San Rafael Barbell membership includes 24/7 door code access. Lift when it fits your life - before sunrise, after midnight, whenever. Your personal code is single-use only. No tailgating.
Space to Focus - Capped Memberships
We cap memberships intentionally. You'll never wait for a rack or a bar. Limited members means you always have the space and equipment you need to work.
Your Program - No Required Classes
Follow your own strength or weightlifting program. No mandatory class attendance, no one correcting your programming choices. Meet you where you are.
Competition-Grade Gear - No Compromises
Competition plates in kg and lb, quality barbells, squat racks with safeties, jerk blocks. The gear a serious lifter actually needs - not a commercial gym approximation of it.
Next Door to CrossFit - Best of Both
Tamalpais CrossFit classes are right next door. San Rafael Barbell members can add coached group classes anytime. Strength work plus community, in one building.

The Setup

EQUIPMENT HIGHLIGHTS

🏋️
Racks & Benches
5 squat racks, competition bench, jerk blocks. Everything you need for Olympic and powerlifting work - no waiting, no sharing required.
⚖️
Plates & Bars
Competition-grade plates in both kg and lb. Quality barbells for all disciplines. No worn-out commercial gym equipment - this is the real thing.
🔩
Dumbbells & Kettlebells
Dumbbells from 5 to 140 lb. Kettlebells from 18 to 98 lb. Full accessory range so you can build the supporting work around your main lifts.
🚴
Conditioning
Rowers, Assault bikes, Concept2 Ski Erg and Ski Bike. Full conditioning tools when you need to build your engine around your strength work.
💪
Strongman & Tools
Tires, battle ropes, and specialty implements for athletes who want to build real-world power. More than a standard gym - a serious training facility.
🏛️
The Environment
Clean, roomy, well-maintained. Shared with people who respect the space. No ego, no noise - just serious people doing serious work.

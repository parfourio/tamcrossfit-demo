Who We Are

Built on Community.
Proven by Results.

"It's a place where the coaching is amazing, I get a great workout, and I can hang out with a wonderful community that supports everyone."

Tamalpais CrossFit has been the home gym of Marin County's most dedicated athletes since 2010. We're not the biggest box - we're the one where coaches know your name, remember your PRs, and won't let you phone it in.

Whether you're stepping into a CrossFit gym for the very first time or you've been competing for years, you'll find your place here. We scale every workout to meet you where you are.
